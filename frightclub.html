Fright Club

What if I had the gumption
to muster up and summon
all of the freakiest creatures
and the evilest demonseed serial killers
and pit the two teams in an epic battle to see
who reigns supreme in dealing nightmarish dreams?


Freddy versus Jason? That'll be a joke
when you compare to the terror of which my rhymes have bespoke.
First up to the plate is Norman Bates a.k.a. the Psycho
vs. that hard St. Bernard that the Cambers named Cujo.
The slasher tries to gash him, but Cujo brings the fight with a bite so ill
it would send all the hounds of the Baskervilles heading for the hills.


Now the next duel may not be at first what it seems;
we have Dracula matched up with the Ghostface killers from Scream.
But the count takes them out with one flash of a fang -
they should have thought to bring Van Helsing in their gang.


As Orlok's clock goes tic-toc that means it's time
for the final battle between Frankenstein and Mr. Hyde.
Look! Dr. Frank sends his monster with a right hook torpedo
that splits Mr. Hyde into a permanent third alter ego.


Now our final tally is 3-0, with monsters in the lead.
They are the reigning Fright Club champions until next Halloween.
