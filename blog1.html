The Plan

July 21, 2017
by Nick


In September I’ll be starting my PhD in Biology at UC San Diego. Rather than be sensible and fly out, my girlfriend Jackie and I are taking a month-long road trip to get there. We’ll be visiting family and friends, hiking national parks, stopping at roadside attractions, and generally seeking out adventure along the way. We’re hoping to share some of our stories through this blog (plus I wanted practice designing another website).

Our intended route is somewhat-Southern, with stops in cities like Charlotte, Nashville, and Austin. If we have time, we hope to catch the August solar eclipse somewhere in its path of totality in Oregon. But if we decide to take our time getting to SD, we can opt for the much shorter route and go straight from the Grand Canyon to our new home.


Wish us safety and patience on our journey!
