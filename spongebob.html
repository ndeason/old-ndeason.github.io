Note: this story was created by feeding transcripts of real Spongebob Squarepants episodes to a predictive text AI. The algorithm, along with my occasional guiding hand, then wrote an original script for a never before seen episode. We present to you...

The Anchovies Who Got Something for Nothing

French Narrator: Ahhh...the sewer that is Bikini Bottom. Everyone is enjoying their Krabby Patties and barnacle fries. Spongebob, too, is enjoying his favorite Saturday morning action: making their jellyfish jelly.


Spongebob Squarepants: Permission to bounce against the ceiling Captain Squidward!


Squidward Tentacles: Oh no you don't. Stop bugging me and go be annoying to Mr. Krabs.


[Spongebob tears himself into another Spongebob and flies off...]


Spongebob: Hey Mr. Krabs! I'm ready to blow bubbles while Squidward looks at me.


Mr. Krabs: Arrgh... Spongebob you think this is a game of treasure hunt don't you lad.


[Patrick Star comes over and challenges Spongebob to make giraffe sounds.]

Mr. Krabs: You boys have your anchors out of your pants.

Patrick: Uhh... I wanna go home to giggle rock.

[Squidward continues to play his nose like a uniquely unshaped clarinet]


* * *


The following morning: a reef blower starts with a large amount of whistling. Spongebob and Patrick run towards Squidward and sit on his head. Squidward narrows his eyelids together and grumbles.

Squidward: You two raspberry pops again. You should be ashamed of yourselves [he laughs backstage].

Spongebob: Come aboard this ship Squidward. Let's search the seaweed mine for treasure!

Mr. Krabs: Did I hears ya say treasure? Dig on! All hands on deck! [Mr. Krabs gives Squidward a wee little plastic salad fork. Squidward looks as if he is a violently depressed Ginsburg.]

Spongebob: I'm sorry Mr. Krabs, I'm not doing the technique. The finest technique ever established for dimwitted sea animals like ducks.

[Patrick gets zapped again. It's dark. Cuts might show in the morning.]

Patrick: Pirate Patrick is number one. Squidward looks like ancient grease!

Mr. Krabs: Pirates, eh? Why are Mr. Sparkles and the Flying Dutchman here? To get me treasure? They should've noticed I can smell the pineapple anchovies from the end of the month!

Squidward: Anchovies? Hah! They dance to tomato techno like all decency has gone silent.

[The anchovies chant dark poetry and the sea floor explodes. Spongebob and Patrick instantly pop and become alive again in cheerleader uniforms. Squidward fades to nothing. Mr. Krabs winks a good bye and the crowd cheers to the moment.

Anchovies: Meep meep!
