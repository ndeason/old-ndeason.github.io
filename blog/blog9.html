The Conclusion

September 24, 2017


As you might have noticed, there were no updates to our blog after Arkansas. The main reason for this is that we decided to enjoy the second half of our road trip without quite as much social media. Also, it's pretty difficult to maintain a website with no internet.

Nevertheless, we still took pictures and wrote in our journals for the remainder of the trip, and these documents will help us cherish this time in our lives forever. As a final goodbye to the blog, we put every single picture we took in a video under 6 minutes. Take a look and feel free to press the pause button for a closer look if something catches your eye.



On our long way to San Diego, we travelled through 11 states, 11 national parks, a handful of national monuments, countless national forests and racked up at least 8000 miles over 28 days. We've found a home that we love in Clairemont, San Diego, and are looking forward to our newest journey...
