A Retrofuturist Manifesto

When I become Supreme Leader of the Galaxy
anyone referring to a self-balancing scooter
as a "hoverboard" will be swiftly executed
by death ray or by phase blaster, I haven't yet decided


In fact all self-balancing scooters
and by extension Segways, will be banned
and in their place will be real hoverboards -
ones that hover - and flying cars


And if you find yourself now thinking "But Supreme Leader,
we already have flying cars. They're called airplanes"
Congratulations
You are now first on a very long list of my Political Enemies
to be executed by death ray or by phase blaster


As your Supreme Leader I will redirect all medical research
toward developing a shrink ray so that diseases
can be fought from the inside-out by doctors the size of germs


And as my Loyal Citizens you will, rest assured, be
quite pleased with the variety of housing options available:
nuke-proof cities in the clouds, nuke-proof underwater glass domes,
and nuke-proof subterranean complexes where, I think,
you'll grok that the Mole People are really quite friendly if
you can stomach all the discussion of fungiculture
